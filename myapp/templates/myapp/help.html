Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create an account?
A: To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top-right corner and fill in your details.

Q: I forgot my password, what should I do?
A: Click on "Forgot Password?" on the login page and follow the instructions to reset your password.

Q: How do I contact customer support?
A: You can reach out to our customer support team through the Contact Us page.

Technical Support

If you are experiencing technical issues, please make sure your browser is up to date or try using a different browser. If the problem persists, contact our technical support team.
