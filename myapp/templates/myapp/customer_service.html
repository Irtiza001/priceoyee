Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track a price?

To track a price, create an account, search for your item, and add it to your wishlist. We'll notify you of any price changes.

Can I receive alerts for price drops?

Yes, you can set up alerts for specific products and we'll notify you via email when the price drops.
